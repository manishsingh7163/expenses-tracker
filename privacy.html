Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 24, 2026

Overview

Expenses Tracker is a free, open-source web application that helps you track income and expenses. Your data is stored exclusively in your own Google Drive account.

Data We Access

When you sign in with Google, the app requests permission to:

  • Google Sheets — To create and manage a spreadsheet called "Expenses Tracker" in your Google Drive where your transactions are stored.
  • Google Drive (limited) — Only to create the spreadsheet file. We cannot see or access any other files in your Drive.
  • Basic profile info — Your name, email, and profile picture, used only to display within the app.

Data Storage

All your financial data (income, expenses, categories, descriptions) is stored only in your own Google Sheet inside your personal Google Drive. We do not have any server or database. No data is sent to or stored on any third-party server.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect, store, or transmit your financial data
  • We do not have access to your Google Drive files
  • We do not use analytics or tracking cookies
  • We do not share any data with third parties
  • We do not run ads

Local Storage

The app stores two small pieces of information in your browser's local storage:

  • Your Google Sheet ID (so the app can find your spreadsheet on return visits)
  • Your basic profile info (name, email) for display purposes

This data stays on your device and is cleared when you sign out.

Open Source

This app is fully open source. You can review the complete source code at github.com/manishsingh7163/expenses-tracker.

Data Deletion

To remove all your data:

  • Sign out from the app (clears local browser data)
  • Delete the "Expenses Tracker" spreadsheet from your Google Drive
  • Revoke app access at myaccount.google.com/permissions

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can reach out via the GitHub Issues page.
